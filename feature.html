Trusted Managed Installer

Our Trusted Managed Installer feature ensures that only approved and verified applications can be installed and run on your Windows systems. By using a trusted installer, we eliminate the risk of unauthorized software compromising your network. This feature includes:

Verification Process: Every application undergoes a rigorous verification process before it is allowed to be installed.
Whitelisting: Only applications that meet your security standards are whitelisted, preventing any unauthorized software from being executed.
Real-time Monitoring: Continuous monitoring ensures that no unverified software slips through the cracks, maintaining the integrity of your systems.

SOE Scanning and Policy Creation

Our Standard Operating Environment (SOE) scanning and policy creation feature simplifies the management of your IT infrastructure. We scan your current environment to create tailored policies that fit your organization's specific needs. This includes:

Comprehensive Scanning: In-depth scans of your current operating environment to identify all applications and processes.
Customized Policies: Creation of bespoke policies based on the unique requirements of your organization, ensuring optimal security and functionality.
Policy Deployment: Seamless deployment of policies across your network, minimizing disruption and maximizing efficiency.

Streamlined Policy Management

Managing security policies can be complex and time-consuming. Our streamlined policy management feature simplifies this process, making it easy to implement and maintain robust security policies. Key components include:

Centralized Management: A single interface for managing all security policies across your organization, providing ease of use and consistency.
Automated Updates: Policies are automatically updated to reflect the latest security standards and organizational changes.
User-Friendly Interface: An intuitive interface that allows for easy policy adjustments and monitoring.

Secure Policy Reversal (OTP)

Our Secure Policy Reversal feature ensures that you can quickly and safely reverse any policy changes if needed. Utilizing a One-Time Password (OTP) system, this feature provides:

Immediate Reversal: Quickly revert to previous policies in case of any issues or unintended consequences.
Secure Access: Use of OTPs ensures that only authorized personnel can initiate a policy reversal, maintaining security even during the reversal process.
Audit Trail: Detailed logs of all policy changes and reversals, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Reduced Cost

Implementing our Windows application control policies not only enhances security but also reduces costs associated with managing your IT infrastructure. Our solutions help you save money through:

Lower Maintenance Costs: Automated and streamlined processes reduce the need for extensive manual oversight and intervention.
Reduced Downtime: Enhanced security and efficient policy management minimize downtime, ensuring continuous productivity.
Optimized Resource Allocation: Efficient use of resources allows your IT team to focus on strategic initiatives rather than routine security tasks.
